4.1 Deformation Of Solids

Hooke's Law

$$ F = kx $$

Stress

$$\sigma = \frac{F}{A}$$

Strain

$$\sigma = \frac{\Delta l}{l_o}$$

Young Modulus

$$E = \frac{Fl}{Ae}$$

Strain energy

$$E_{strain} = \frac{1}{2}Kx^2$$

4.2 Thermal Physics

Boyle's Law

$$P \propto \frac{1}{V}$$

Charles Law

$$V \propto T$$

Gay-Lussac/Pressure Law

$$P \propto T$$

Combined Gas Law

$$\frac{PV}{T} = K$$

Closed System

$$\frac{P_1V_1}{T_1} = \frac{P_2V_2}{T_2}$$

Combined Gas Laws

$$ PV = NkT $$ $$ PV = nAKT$$ $$ PV = nRT $$

Thermal Energy Equation

$$ PV = \frac{1}{3}Nm< c^2 >$$

Specific Heat Capacity

$$\Delta{Q} = mc\Delta\theta$$

4.3 Uniform Circular Motion

Angular Velocity

$$ \omega = \frac{\theta}{t}$$ $$ \theta = \frac{s}{t}$$ $$ \omega = \frac{2\pi}{T}$$ $$ \omega = 2\pi f $$

Tangential Velocity

$$ V = r\omega $$

Acceleration

$$ a = \frac{v^2}{r} $$ $$ a = r\omega^2 $$

Forces

$$F = \frac{mv^2}{r}$$ $$F = mr\omega^2$$

4.4 Simple Harmonic Motion

$$\cos{\theta} = \frac{x}{A}$$ $$x = A\cos({\omega}t)$$ $$a = -\omega^{2}x$$

Free Vibration

$$f_o = \frac{1}{T}$$ $$T = \frac{2\pi}{\omega}$$ $$f_o = \frac{\omega}{2\pi}$$

4.5 The Nucleus

Radius of a Nucleus

$$r = r_oA^\frac{1}{3}$$

Number of radioactive nuclei present

$$N_t = N_oe^{\lambda t}$$

Number of Activity

$$A_t = A_oe^{\lambda t}$$

Current

$$I_t = I_oe^{\lambda t}$$

4.6 Nuclear Decay

Half Life

$$t_{\frac{1}{2}} = \frac{0.693}{\lambda}$$

4.7 Nuclear Energy

Einstien's Equation

$$E = \delta mc^2$$
